I'm Blaine Pace

Hello! Welcome to my Jekyll-powered blog, where I write about things generally related to programming and software development. My name's Blaine Pace, I'm a student at California State University, Chico and a part-time professional Ruby on Rails developer.

I put this blog together to learn more about mobile responsive design in ZURB Foundation and to share projects I've worked on. I'm a FOSS proponent and like to talk about different Linux distributions, and share common problems I come across using libre software.

I've been known to do some work from time to time with hardware platforms such as Arduino. You might also see a post or two about my hardware experiments when I find time to pick up a soldering iron.
